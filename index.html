FAQ

Common questions

Is NexBill POS free to download?

Yes! NexBill POS is completely free to download and includes a free trial period. A subscription is required after the trial to continue using all features.

Is NexBill POS GST compliant?

Yes. NexBill POS automatically calculates CGST, SGST, and IGST based on your product tax settings and generates fully GST-compliant invoices.

Can I share invoices on WhatsApp?

Yes! After generating an invoice, you can share it directly to a customer's WhatsApp number with one click. The invoice is sent as a PDF or link.

What if Windows SmartScreen blocks installation?

This is normal for newly published apps. When SmartScreen appears, click "More info" and then "Run anyway". The app is completely safe — this warning appears because we are a new publisher.

Can multiple people use it at the same shop?

Yes. NexBill POS supports multi-user login. Your shop owner and staff can each have their own login while sharing the same business data.

Does it work offline?

NexBill POS requires an internet connection to sync data to the cloud. A stable connection is recommended for the best experience during billing operations.
